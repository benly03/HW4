Risk Factor Analysis of Peritoneal Carcinomatosis for Young Adults

Full Project Description

My research project aims to do a risk factor analysis for young adults with peritoneal carcinomatosis. Peritoneal carcinomatosis is cancer developing in the abdominal membrane that lines its organs. Aiming to identify plausible risk factors for early onset pertioneal carcinomatosis with patient data from NCDB 2004-2020 database. Main tools used were R-Studio for running analysis and univariable and multivariable logistic regression for predictive analysis. The biggest challenge in completing this project was learning how to use R-Studio effectively and efficiently in the short 10 week duration of the internship. More specifically learning how to manipulate databases correctly so as to not lose important data points or overlook potential variables/risk factors.

Research was supported by UCSD HSI-DDI

Tools used:

  • R-Studio
  • Microsoft powerpoint
  • Excel

Analysis method:

  • Univariable Logistic regression
  • Multivariable Logistic regression

Results

Risk factor analysis showed many factors could possibly effect early development of Carcinomatosis but main risk factors include;

  1. Increasing age
  2. Black race
  3. Private insurance
  4. Increasing tumor stage
